Full Disclaimer

Not therapy or clinical care

mentAImage™ is a preventive mental wellness platform. PulseKey™ and Monai™ are not therapy, counselling, psychotherapy, or clinical mental health treatment. Nothing on this platform constitutes a medical or psychiatric diagnosis, clinical assessment, or treatment recommendation.

Not a crisis service

mentAImage™ and Monai™ are not crisis services. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, thoughts of self-harm or suicide, or any emergency, do not rely on this platform. Call or text 9-8-8 (Canada Suicide Crisis Helpline, available 24/7), call 911, or go to the nearest emergency department.

About Monai

Monai™ is an artificial intelligence system. You are not speaking with a human therapist, psychologist, counsellor, or crisis responder. Monai™ does not diagnose, prescribe, treat, or replace professional care. Monai™ is designed to support reflection and coping-skills practice only.

Employer data policy

When PulseKey™ is deployed through an employer or organization, no individual employee check-in content is ever shared with the employer. Employers and HR administrators see only aggregated, de-identified group-level trend data. Individual mental health information is never used in employment decisions, performance reviews, or disciplinary processes. No individual employee data is shared with employers - ever.

Data and privacy

Your personal data is stored in Canada, encrypted at rest, and is never sold to third parties. You may request deletion of your data at any time. See our Privacy Policy.

What Monai™ does

Reflection. Psychoeducation. Coping skills.

Reflect

Monai™ reads your PulseKey™ mental wellness trends and current check-in mental wellness signals to offer contextual reflections. It knows how you have been doing over time, not just today.

Evidence-based resources

Every suggestion Monai™ makes is grounded in peer-reviewed mental wellness and stress management research: breathing techniques, grounding exercises, cognitive reframing, and validated coping strategies. Monai™ does not draw from general internet content or commercial AI training data.

Real-time support

Monai™ is available between check-ins for on-the-go support. Feeling overwhelmed before a meeting? Monai™ can walk you through a validated 3-minute regulation exercise right then.

Navigate

When you need more than Monai™ can offer, Monai™ helps you find the right next step toward professional support.

What makes Monai™ different

Depth over breadth. Your data, not a script.

Monai™ is not a general-purpose conversational AI. It does not have opinions about the news, cannot help you write emails, and has not been trained on the entire internet. Behind Monai™ is a multimodal AI system that draws on your voice acoustics, written responses, and drawing signals together, not just your words. Monai™ knows one domain deeply: evidence-based mental wellness and stress management science. Every recommendation it makes traces back to a validated source.

Monai™ also has something most AI companions do not: your history. It can see your PulseKey™ mental wellness trends, notice when patterns are shifting, and offer support that is grounded in your actual data, not a generic script.

The space between

For the moments between professional care

Monai™ is not meant to replace your therapist, doctor, or counsellor. It is for the moments in between: when you want to reflect, practice a skill, or have a structured space to think.

If you are already working with a professional, Monai™ can help you prepare for appointments, practice what you are learning, and track how you are doing.
