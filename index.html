About PulseKey™

A 3-minute weekly window into your wellbeing

PulseKey™ is a 3-minute weekly multimodal check-in combining voice, structured and free-form text, and optional drawing or handwriting. AI analysis across all three channels builds a personalized picture of how your mental wellness and stress management capacity shift over time, giving you actionable, non-diagnostic insights grounded in evidence.

Features

Three modalities. One complete picture.

Voice

Speak a short phrase. AI analysis of your voice acoustics captures pitch, rhythm, and energy patterns that reflect your mental state in ways words often cannot. This channel is especially valuable for anyone whose stress does not show up in what they say.

Text

A structured question anchors each check-in, followed by a free-form space to write whatever is on your mind. Both are analyzed to add context and self-awareness to the picture.

Drawing and handwriting

An optional canvas captures drawing and handwriting as non-verbal expression. For neurodivergent individuals and anyone who processes stress visually, this modality catches signals that verbal tools miss entirely.

Personal trends over time

Three minutes a week builds a personal baseline. PulseKey™ tracks how your mental wellness signals change - not against a population norm, but against your own patterns.

Why multimodal?

One channel is not enough

Most wellness tools rely on one thing: what you say. PulseKey™ uses three channels. Voice acoustics, written reflection, and visual expression together produce a richer, more accurate signal than any single modality. This matters especially for the 1 in 5 people who identify as neurodivergent, many of whom express stress non-verbally. PulseKey™ is designed to hear all of it. PulseKey™'s AI engine performs multimodal signal analysis across all three channels simultaneously, surfacing patterns that no single-channel tool can see.

What Monai™ does

Reflection. Psychoeducation. Coping skills.

Evidence-based resources

Every suggestion Monai™ makes is grounded in peer-reviewed mental wellness and stress management research: breathing techniques, grounding exercises, cognitive reframing, and validated coping strategies. Monai™ does not draw from general internet content or commercial AI training data.

Navigate

When you need more than Monai™ can offer, Monai™ helps you find the right next step toward professional support.

What makes Monai™ different

Depth over breadth. Your data, not a script.

Monai™ knows one domain deeply: evidence-based mental wellness and stress management science. Every recommendation it makes traces back to a validated source.

Monai™ also has something most AI companions do not: your history. It can see your PulseKey™ mental wellness trends, notice when patterns are shifting, and offer support that is grounded in your actual data, not a generic script.

Our Story

Built at the intersection of medicine, AI, and human behavior

Most wellness tools ask you how you feel and leave it at that. We knew that wasn’t enough. mentAImage™ was built to capture what words alone cannot, and to give everyone a clearer picture of how they are really doing.

The Founder

Dr. Saber Ghadakzadeh, MD-PhD

Dr. Ghadakzadeh is a physician scientist and entrepreneur with an MD, MSc, and PhD. Over 25 years, he has worked across clinical medicine, academic research, genetic engineering, nanotechnology, and AI in healthcare. He is the founder of mentAImage™ and PulseKey™.

Working across hospitals, research labs, and organizations, he kept noticing the same thing: stress and burnout were building up long before anyone knew. Surveys were not working. People were afraid to speak honestly. And the signals that mattered most, voice, expression, non-verbal behavior, were never captured at all.

He built mentAImage™ to fill that gap. Not another survey. Not another chatbot that asks how you feel. A multimodal system that listens, observes, and understands, and keeps everything private by design.

“If someone doesn’t understand something, maybe it’s not being explained in the right way. That philosophy shaped everything we built.”

Why mentAImage™

The problems we set out to solve

Burnout is not a personal failure. It builds upstream, quietly, over months. By the time it shows up as a disability claim or a resignation, it is already too late. We built tools to surface those signals earlier, for everyone.

Surveys do not work

People are afraid to speak up. Engagement is low because people fear being targeted. We built a check-in system people can trust, with individual data that never reaches employers without informed consent.

One channel is not enough

What someone writes in a text box only captures what they can articulate. Voice, drawing, and writing together reveal patterns that words alone cannot. Our tools are multimodal by design.

Wellness should not depend on one person

A passionate HR manager who leaves takes all the progress with them. We help organizations build wellness into their systems, their hiring, their onboarding, their processes, so it outlasts any individual.

Who we serve

Built for everyone. Especially those the standard tools miss.

mentAImage™ is designed for anyone who experiences stress. That includes every person in every workplace. We are particularly effective for neurodivergent individuals, not because they need a special tool, but because standard wellness tools were not built with them in mind.

Neurodivergent people often communicate differently, process stress differently, and express themselves through channels that conventional check-ins never capture. Our multimodal approach, combining voice acoustics, written reflection, and creative expression, picks up signals that a text box simply cannot. That makes mentAImage™ more accurate for everyone, and genuinely useful for those who have been invisible to other tools.

Supporting neurodivergent individuals well is also not a one-way street. It requires building awareness across entire teams, not just accommodating one person. Our organizational tools help leaders understand their teams, not just the individuals who feel safe enough to disclose.

Notre histoire

Construit à l’intersection de la médecine, de l’IA et du comportement humain

La plupart des outils de mieux-être vous demandent comment vous vous sentez et s’arrêtent là. Nous savions que ce n’était pas suffisant. mentAImage™ a été construit pour capter ce que les mots seuls ne peuvent pas.

Le fondateur

Dr Saber Ghadakzadeh, MD-PhD

Le Dr Ghadakzadeh est médecin-chercheur et entrepreneur titulaire d’un MD, d’une Maîtrise et d’un PhD. Au cours de 25 ans, il a travaillé en médecine clinique, recherche académique, génie génétique, nanotechnologie et IA en santé. Il est le fondateur de mentAImage™ et PulseKey™.

En travaillant dans les hôpitaux, les laboratoires de recherche et les organisations, il a constamment observé la même chose: le stress et l’épuisement s’accumulaient bien avant que quiconque ne le sache. Les sondages ne fonctionnaient pas. Les gens avaient peur de parler honnêtement. Et les signaux les plus importants, la voix, l’expression, le comportement non verbal, n’étaient jamais capturés.

Il a construit mentAImage™ pour combler cette lacune. Pas un autre sondage. Pas un autre chatbot qui demande comment vous vous sentez. Un système multimodal qui écoute, observe et comprend, tout en gardant tout privé par conception.

« Si quelqu’un ne comprend pas quelque chose, c’est peut-être qu’on ne l’explique pas de la bonne façon. Cette philosophie a façonné tout ce que nous avons construit. »

Pourquoi mentAImage™

Les problèmes que nous avons voulu résoudre

L’épuisement professionnel n’est pas un échec personnel. Il se construit en amont, silencieusement, sur des mois. Nous avons construit des outils pour faire remonter ces signaux plus tôt.

Les sondages ne fonctionnent pas

Les gens ont peur de s’exprimer. La participation est faible car les gens craignent d’être ciblés. Nous avons construit un système de bilan en lequel les gens peuvent avoir confiance.

Un seul canal ne suffit pas

Ce que quelqu’un écrit dans une zone de texte ne capte que ce qu’il peut articuler. La voix, le dessin et l’écriture ensemble révèlent des schémas que les mots seuls ne peuvent pas.

Le mieux-être ne devrait pas dépendre d’une seule personne

Un responsable RH passionné qui part emporte tous les progrès avec lui. Nous aidons les organisations à intégrer le mieux-être dans leurs systèmes, leur embauche, leur intégration et leurs processus.

À qui nous nous adressons

Conçu pour tout le monde. Surtout ceux que les outils standard ratent.

mentAImage™ est conçu pour toute personne qui vit du stress, ce qui inclut chaque individu dans chaque milieu de travail. Nous sommes particulièrement efficaces pour les personnes neurodiverses, non pas parce qu’elles ont besoin d’un outil spécial, mais parce que les outils de mieux-être standard n’ont pas été conçus en les gardant à l’esprit.

Notre approche multimodale, combinant les acoustiques vocales, la réflexion écrite et l’expression créative, capte des signaux qu’une zone de texte ne peut tout simplement pas. Cela rend mentAImage™ plus précis pour tout le monde, et véritablement utile pour ceux qui ont été invisibles aux autres outils.
