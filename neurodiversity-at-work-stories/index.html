Beneath the Resume — A mentAImage Series

Neurodiversity at Work

Real stories of neurodivergent professionals navigating workplaces, finding their strengths, and advocating for inclusion. Curated and written by Elissia Moullas.

About this series

These are the stories that rarely appear on a resume. They are stories of burnout and resilience, of diagnosis and clarity, of workplaces that failed and workplaces that transformed. Each person here shared their experience so that others would feel less alone, and so that leaders might build something better. We are grateful to every contributor who trusted us with their story.
