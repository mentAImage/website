Full Disclaimer

Not therapy or clinical care

mentAImage™ is a preventive mental wellness platform. PulseKey™ and Monai™ are not therapy, counselling, psychotherapy, or clinical mental health treatment. Nothing on this platform constitutes a medical or psychiatric diagnosis, clinical assessment, or treatment recommendation.

Not a crisis service

mentAImage™ and Monai™ are not crisis services. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, thoughts of self-harm or suicide, or any emergency, do not rely on this platform. Call or text 9-8-8 (Canada Suicide Crisis Helpline, available 24/7), call 911, or go to the nearest emergency department.

About Monai

Monai™ is an artificial intelligence system. You are not speaking with a human therapist, psychologist, counsellor, or crisis responder. Monai™ does not diagnose, prescribe, treat, or replace professional care. Monai™ is designed to support reflection and coping-skills practice only.

Employer data policy

When PulseKey™ is deployed through an employer or organization, no individual employee check-in content is ever shared with the employer. Employers and HR administrators see only aggregated, de-identified group-level trend data. Individual mental health information is never used in employment decisions, performance reviews, or disciplinary processes. No individual employee data is shared with employers - ever.

Data and privacy

Your personal data is stored in Canada, encrypted at rest, and is never sold to third parties. You may request deletion of your data at any time. See our Privacy Policy.

Our Story

Built at the intersection of medicine, AI, and human behavior

Most wellness tools ask you how you feel and leave it at that. We knew that wasn’t enough. mentAImage™ was built to capture what words alone cannot, and to give everyone a clearer picture of how they are really doing.

The Founder

Dr. Saber Ghadakzadeh, MD-PhD

Dr. Ghadakzadeh is a physician scientist and entrepreneur with an MD, MSc, and PhD. Over 25 years, he has worked across clinical medicine, academic research, genetic engineering, nanotechnology, and AI in healthcare. He is the founder of mentAImage™ and PulseKey™.

Working across hospitals, research labs, and organizations, he kept noticing the same thing: stress and burnout were building up long before anyone knew. Surveys were not working. People were afraid to speak honestly. And the signals that mattered most, voice, expression, non-verbal behavior, were never captured at all.

He built mentAImage™ to fill that gap. Not another survey. Not another chatbot that asks how you feel. A multimodal system that listens, observes, and understands, and keeps everything private by design.

“If someone doesn’t understand something, maybe it’s not being explained in the right way. That philosophy shaped everything we built.”

Why mentAImage™

The problems we set out to solve

Burnout is not a personal failure. It builds upstream, quietly, over months. By the time it shows up as a disability claim or a resignation, it is already too late. We built tools to surface those signals earlier, for everyone.

Surveys do not work

People are afraid to speak up. Engagement is low because people fear being targeted. We built a check-in system people can trust, with individual data that never reaches employers without informed consent.

One channel is not enough

What someone writes in a text box only captures what they can articulate. Voice, drawing, and writing together reveal patterns that words alone cannot. Our tools are multimodal by design.

Wellness should not depend on one person

A passionate HR manager who leaves takes all the progress with them. We help organizations build wellness into their systems, their hiring, their onboarding, their processes, so it outlasts any individual.

Who we serve

Built for everyone. Especially those the standard tools miss.

mentAImage™ is designed for anyone who experiences stress. That includes every person in every workplace. We are particularly effective for neurodivergent individuals, not because they need a special tool, but because standard wellness tools were not built with them in mind.

Neurodivergent people often communicate differently, process stress differently, and express themselves through channels that conventional check-ins never capture. Our multimodal approach, combining voice acoustics, written reflection, and creative expression, picks up signals that a text box simply cannot. That makes mentAImage™ more accurate for everyone, and genuinely useful for those who have been invisible to other tools.

Supporting neurodivergent individuals well is also not a one-way street. It requires building awareness across entire teams, not just accommodating one person. Our organizational tools help leaders understand their teams, not just the individuals who feel safe enough to disclose.
